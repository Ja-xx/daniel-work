Who we are:

Academic Excellence

ESCT UNIVERSITY, BÉNIN (ECOLE SUPIERIEUR DES CADRES ET TECHNICIENS ) is a private university of Management and Technology. ESCT university is in to becoming one of the best university in the 21st century. The University is widely recognized for the quality of its undergraduate, graduate and professional programs. ESCT university ensures the quality of its programs through careful focusing of its resources in areas of institutional strengths.

A University with a long history

ESCT UNIVERSITY, BENIN. (ECOLE SUPIERIEURE DES CADRES ET TECH-NICIENS) was founded and established in 1992 by Prof. Adingni Francois, Founder, holder of the Higher Education Diploma Specializing in Project Management and Technicians. It was accredited same year to offer certificates, diplomas, undergraduate degrees and postgraduate programmes by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research (MHESR) in Republic of Benin.

Pioneering Approach

The university, whose mission is to train senior managers in the fields of Management and Technology, to expand the frontiers of knowledge through provision of excellent conditions for learning and research for societal response, is located Opposite Zongo-Mosque, Djeregbe, Republique du Benin.
Our training is based on a pedagogical approach alternating teaching and internship.
In ESCT UNIVERSITY, we say, “Explore your interests, chase your ideas, and find out what drives and excites you”!.

Features

Secure User Authentication

To ensure the security of student information, our online clearance system employs robust user authentication mechanisms. Students can securely log in using their unique credentials, safeguarding their personal data throughout the clearance process.

24/7 Accessibility

Our online clearance system is accessible 24/7, providing students with the flexibility to complete their clearance tasks at their own convenience. Whether it's during office hours or late at night, students can access the system whenever it suits their schedule, enhancing flexibility and accessibility.

Document Upload Capability

Students can conveniently upload required documents directly through the online clearance system. Whether it's academic transcripts, proof of immunization, or financial aid documentation, the document upload feature allows students to submit necessary paperwork with ease, reducing administrative burden.

User-Friendly Design

With a user-friendly design and intuitive navigation, our online clearance system is easy to use for students of all technical backgrounds. Clear instructions and prompts guide students through each step of the clearance process, ensuring a seamless user experience.
