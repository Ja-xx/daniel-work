FAQ

What is online clearance at ESCT University?

Online clearance at ESCT University is the process through which newly admitted students complete various requirements for enrollment, such as submitting necessary documents and paying fees, all online.

 How do I access the online clearance portal at ESCT University?

You can access the online clearance portal at ESCT Univesity by logging into your student account on the university website and following the instructions provided.

What documents do I need to submit for online clearance at ESCT University?

You will need to submit your admission letter, proof of payment of fees, identification documents, and any other documents requested by the university.

Can I complete online clearance before arriving on campus?

Yes, online clearance can be completed before arriving on campus to ensure a smooth enrollment process once you arrive.

Is there a deadline for completing online clearance at ESCT University?

Yes, there is a deadline for completing online clearance, which will be communicated to you upon admission.
